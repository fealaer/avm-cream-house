My name is Andrey Pushkarev.

I am a software engineer, traveler, surfer and the creator of this application.

During my trip to Sri Lanka, I spent a lot of time around Weligama and often visited the cafe AVM Cream House. At one day, I realized that it was difficult to pick a new juice to try, my friends had the same problem, because:

- there are more than 60 fruits in the menu, most of them many people do not know;

- there are more than 500 drinks in the menu based on local fruits.

Thus, I got the idea to make this application.

I developed this application as an open source, so you can find complete source code on Github. I will highly appreciate if somebody decide to contribute to this application.

I am looking forward to receive feedback and suggestions from you.

I hope that the application AVM Cream House will help you to choose your next juice and discover new fruits!
